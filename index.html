What to try in Tunisia: TOP-2 national dishes

The national cuisine of Tunisia is very interesting. Culinary traditions are similar to French ones in many ways, but the portions are generous in an oriental way, with a bright mix of aromatic spices. Let's tell you what is worth trying in Tunisia in the first place. And we will advise you what to buy home to repeat local dishes. Learning about the national dishes of Tunisia will not hurt even tourists vacationing in all-inclusive hotels. Photos with a description of the dishes will help you figure out what is being fed at the buffet. We have compiled the top 10 main culinary masterpieces of this hospitable country: hot dishes, snacks, desserts - for every taste. Do not be afraid to relax in Tunisia on the "breakfast + dinner" or simply "breakfast" system. Food prices in Hammamet, Sousse, Monastir or Djerba Island will be low. The portions are generous, they cook delicious. For example, for a lunch of fish with salad + a drink in many restaurants in Tunisia, you will give about 5-6 dollars.

Tajin

In many Arab countries, this is the name of a roast cooked in a ceramic pot. Tunisian tajine is not like that at all. This national dish is more like a meat omelette. Lamb or chicken is finely chopped, mixed with potatoes or chickpeas, poured with eggs and cheese. This dish in Tunisia is worth trying in hot and cold versions, they are both good. In hotels, tajine can be found in the side dishes section. In Tunisia, you can buy a ceramic pot "tajine" to cook the dish of the same name at home.

Brick

Be sure to try a delicious fried cake made of thin puff pastry in Tunisia. The filling is eggs with tuna, yams with spices, meat, shrimp, sometimes spinach with cheese or something else. Brick is a cross between chebureks and deep–fried pancakes with stuffing. In Djerba, they like to cook this pie with harissa, it will turn out sharp. Brick is often served with a mix of vegetables and salted lemons. The latter can be found in supermarkets in glass jars. It's hard to get them in Russia, take Tunisian ones. To make a brick at home, you can bring home special Feuilles de Brick cakes. They somewhat resemble pita bread, are sold in the same packages, in supermarkets they lie in the bread department.
